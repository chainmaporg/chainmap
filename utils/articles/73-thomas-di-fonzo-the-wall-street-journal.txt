What Can You Buy With Bitcoin: A $10 Pizza for $76?
A video on the challenges of spending the virtual currency on the streets of New York
Bitcoin is a virtual currency, but very few people use it to actually pay for things because of transaction fees and its rising value. WSJ's Thomas Di Fonzo takes to the streets of New York to try to spend bitcoin at brick-and-mortar establishments. Watch the above video without a subscription.
